R packages

  • RVPA: VPAを用いた日本国内資源の資源評価を行うためのRのパッケージ

  • doRLP: Operating model to test performance of integrated model of Stock Synthesis (SS) and do relative likelihood profiles (RLP) for the simulated replications.

  • glm.tree: For creating area stratification automatically in conducting glm.

  • sstools: For creating graphical diagnostics with Stock Synthesis (SS) output.

  • ssfuture: For conducting stochastic future projections from Stock Synthesis (SS) output.
      Beta version is uploaded (30th May 2011). However, man and manuals are only preliminary.
